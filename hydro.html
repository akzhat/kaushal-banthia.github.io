Welcome to Green Box. . .



We at Green Box believe that food should be grown in
a sustainable manner. While growing the crops, we not
only take care of the taste of the vegetables, but also
think about our environment. Hence, Green Box uses a
modern technique of farming, namely Hydroponics.
Using this method, we consume upto 70-90% less
water than traditional soil based farming.

Let's Save Water...

And also grow tasty vegetables along with it . . .
